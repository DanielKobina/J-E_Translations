Jumping from games into a theater near you, the creator of Mario speaks


Super Mario Bros was released for the first time, 38 years ago.

Giving rise to an unprecedented boom, even now Mario is loved around the world, 
but this year he will leap from the gaming world and onto the big screen.

We asked Shigeru Miyamoto, the creator of Mario and the associate producer of this work
about the development of Mario's secret tale and his thoughts on the upcoming movie.
